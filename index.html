Flóttaleikr: A Tafl Game

[ˈflou̯t.taˌlei̯kr̩]

Rules

Flóttaleikr is an asymmetric escape game. The inner player wins if they get their center pawn to the edge of the board. The outer player wins if they capture the center pawn.

On your turn you must do exactly one of the following:
  1. Move any two of your pieces any number of tiles in a straight line.
  2. Move the center piece once any number of tiles in a straight line.
  3. Capture one tile diagonally with displacement.
